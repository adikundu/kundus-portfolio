I am Aditya Kundu. A CS undergrad from SRM IST, Chennai, India.

This portfolio features a few design projects that I have undertaken for several college teams and aspirational startups while pursuing my undergraduate degree in Computer Science.

Working within such modest organizations comprising of dedicated individuals using an all-hands-on-deck type of approach to things, I was introduced to a multitude of approaches and technologies as I tried to find my way through them.

A core part of my design approach is to consider the experience that people go through while using a product by weighing how meaningful each interaction with the product/brand is.

Those little moments of delight are what truly make a product successful; and are exactly the details that I have tried to explain within the showcased projects.

In addition to my work, this website is meant to exhibit my personality as I perceive it to be. The site is filled with fun and quirky elements and language that I unapologetically use and adhere to.

I also absolutely love to play video games. Experiencing stories rather than reading, listening or hearing them is an irreplacable experience.

Following that, the website is also filled with several interactive Easter Eggs that I urge all viewers to discover and enjoy!
